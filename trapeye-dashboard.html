Scam Watch
CRITICAL
🎣 University Phishing Email
Fake emails impersonating university admin demanding you "verify your account" or lose access.
phishingemailimpersonation
Red Flags
  • Sender email doesn't match official domain
  • Creates extreme urgency ("24 hours")
  • Link URL doesn't match university site
  • Asks for password directly
How to Avoid
  • Never click links — go directly to the site
  • Verify with IT department directly
  • Enable 2FA on your student account
CRITICAL
💼 Fake Internship Offer
Too-good-to-be-true remote internships asking you to pay for "training kits" or share bank details for "salary setup".
jobfinancialsocial-eng
Red Flags
  • Hired without proper interview
  • Company can't be found on LinkedIn
  • Asks you to buy equipment first
  • Payment via UPI to personal numbers
How to Avoid
  • Verify company on official websites
  • Never pay to get a job
  • Use official campus placement portals
HIGH
📱 QR Code Payment Fraud
Malicious QR codes placed on canteen tables, hostel noticeboards that redirect to phishing payment pages.
qr-codeupipayment
Red Flags
  • QR code sticker pasted over original
  • URL after scan looks odd
  • Asks you to enter UPI PIN to "receive" money
How to Avoid
  • Always preview URL before opening
  • Use QR scanner with link preview
  • PIN is only needed to send, not receive
HIGH
🎓 Scholarship / Loan Scam
Fraudulent WhatsApp/Telegram messages offering government scholarships requiring registration fees or Aadhaar upload.
scholarshipgovt-impersonationidentity
Red Flags
  • Unofficial WhatsApp/Telegram source
  • Requires upfront "processing fee"
  • Asks for Aadhaar or bank OTP
How to Avoid
  • Check only NSP (scholarships.gov.in)
  • Never share Aadhaar on unofficial platforms
  • Real scholarships never ask for fees
CRITICAL
🔢 OTP / SIM Swap Fraud
Scammer calls pretending to be bank/telecom support, tricks you into sharing OTP to "prevent account deactivation".
otpsim-swapvishing
Red Flags
  • Unexpected call about account issue
  • Pressure to share OTP "within 2 minutes"
  • Caller knows partial account details (social engineering)
How to Avoid
  • Banks NEVER ask for OTP via phone
  • Hang up and call bank's official number
  • Block SIM if you get deactivation SMSes
MEDIUM
💔 Romance / Sextortion Scam
Fake profiles on dating apps / Instagram build emotional connection, then request money or threaten to leak intimate content.
social-mediasextortionblackmail
Red Flags
  • Profile photos look too perfect (AI-generated)
  • Refuses video calls or makes excuses
  • Sudden financial emergency after trust is built
How to Avoid
  • Reverse image search profile photos
  • Never send money to online strangers
  • Report sextortion to cybercrime.gov.in immediately
HIGH
📲 Fake Banking / UPI App
APK files shared via WhatsApp mimicking BHIM, Google Pay, or popular banking apps that steal credentials.
malwareapkcredential-theft
Red Flags
  • Shared as APK, not via Play Store
  • Asks for excessive permissions
  • UI looks slightly different from original
How to Avoid
  • Only install apps from official stores
  • Disable "install from unknown sources"
  • Check app permissions before installing
MEDIUM
🎰 Lottery / Prize Scam
"You've won ₹50,000!" SMS/emails requiring you to pay taxes or fees first to claim non-existent prize money.
lotteryadvance-feesms
How to Avoid
  • You can't win a contest you didn't enter
  • Real prizes never require upfront fees
  • Ignore and delete such messages
MEDIUM
👁 Cyber Stalking & Doxxing
Harassers collect publicly available personal information to intimidate, threaten, or expose victims online.
privacyharassmentsocial-media
How to Avoid
  • Audit your social media privacy settings
  • Never post real-time location
  • Use a pseudonym on public forums
  • Report to cybercrime.gov.in & NCRB

If you've been scammed or face cyber threat, dial 1930 (National Cybercrime Helpline) immediately. Available 24×7.
⬢ Reporting Authorities — India
🏛
National Cybercrime Portal
cybercrime.gov.in
Official Government of India portal. File complaints for financial fraud, child abuse, social media crimes, and all cybercrimes. Available 24/7.
📞
Cyber Crime Helpline
Helpline: 1930
Call 1930 for immediate assistance with online financial frauds. Quick response team that can alert banks to freeze transactions.
🏦
Reserve Bank of India
bankingombudsman.rbi.org.in
Report banking fraud, unauthorized transactions, UPI scams, and online payment frauds. RBI Ombudsman handles bank-related cybercrimes.
📡
CERT-In
cert-in.org.in · incident@cert-in.org.in
Indian Computer Emergency Response Team. Report malware, phishing websites, data breaches, and critical infrastructure attacks.
🚔
Local Cyber Police Cell
cybercrime.gov.in/Locateofficer.aspx
File FIR at your nearest cyber police station. Use the portal to locate your jurisdiction's cybercrime unit for in-person reporting.
📱
Sanchar Saathi (DoT)
sancharsaathi.gov.in · Helpline: 1800-11-0420
Report mobile fraud, fake SIM cards, and telecom-related scams. Block stolen phones and report misuse of your phone number.
✅ Steps to Take Immediately After a Scam
1
Call 1930 immediately
Report financial fraud within the first hour for best chance of recovery
2
Change all passwords
Email, banking, and social media accounts — use a fresh device if possible
3
Screenshot everything
Preserve evidence: messages, transaction IDs, scammer profiles
4
File on cybercrime.gov.in
Submit formal complaint with all collected evidence
5
Notify your bank
Request chargeback or transaction block; report card for reissuance
6
Run a malware scan
If you installed anything suspicious, scan all devices immediately
7
Tell someone you trust
Scams cause stress — tell a friend, family, or counsellor
8
Warn others in your college group
Share scam details (without personal info) to protect peers
